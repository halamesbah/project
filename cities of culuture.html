TOP 5 HISTORICAL CITIES

The Colosseum , An Ancient Roman Amphitheatre

Located in Rome, Italy, the Colosseum is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Rome.
Also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, it is an iconic landmark in Rome and one of the most recognizable symbols of ancient Roman engineering and architecture.
It allows visitors to step back in time and live out their gladiator fantasies (a man who fights against another man or a wild animal in a public show),
as well as offer an insight into the history of the ancient Romans. Definitely worth including in the list of one of the most famous historical places in the world.

Best time to visit:

April to September(when the weather is sunny and pleasant)

Best things to do:

1.The Colosseum is one of the most iconic landmarks in Rome, and no visit to the city would be complete without experiencing its historical significance. 2. Explore the Underground: The Colosseums underground chambers are a fascinating glimpse of the fighter men of that period. 3.There are many different Colosseum tours available, ranging from general tours to more specialised tours that focus on specific aspects of the Colosseum, such as its history, architecture, or engineering.

The Taj Mahal ,Symbol of love

Since you are looking for historical places in the world, then no one can forget this majestic beauty “The Taj Mahal”,
located in India it is an ivory-white marble mausoleum that was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
It is a symbol of love and devotion. It took 22 years to build, from 1632 to 1653. Over 22,000 workers were involved in its construction. The Taj Mahal is said to change color depending on the time of day. In the morning, it appears pink, while in the evening, it appears white. The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous and historical places in India. I hope this gives you a better idea of why the Taj Mahal is such a special place. So If you want to witness this living beauty, then you should definitely plan a tour to this destination which is one of the most pleasing historical monuments in the world. The Taj Mahal is one of the most visited historical places in India.

Best time to visit

October to March

Best things to do

the Tomb inside the monument, where you can experience the remarkable interior design. Don’t forget to watch a soothing sunset view.

Angkor Wat , A Sprawling Temple Complex

Sometimes referred to as “the Eighth Wonder of the World.”Angkor Wat, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the largest religious structure in the world is a sprawling temple complex located in northern Cambodia.
Angkor Wat literally translates to City of Temples.One of the historical places in the world,.
it was built in the 12th century by King Suryavarman II. The complex was originally dedicated to the Hindu God Vishnu but gradually transformed into a Buddhist temple towards the end of the 12th century..
It is one of the historical monuments of the world with a religious touch. This historical place is also featured in Cambodia’s national currency, the Cambodian riel.

Best time to visit:

You can visit this throughout the year, but during the rainy season June to October, the whole of Angkor Wat is quieter,.
so it is the best time to avoid the crowds. In the rainy season, the scenery is mesmerising,.
but it’s most rainy in the afternoon, so it’s better to visit in the morning, making it one of the most photographed places in the world..

Best things to do :

1.Exploring the temples 2.Watching the sunset at Phnom Bakheng (a sunset point) 3.Taking a ride in tuk-tuk 4.Look at the wall art that tells the story of Kurukshetra battles 5.Spending a night in Siem Reap (a place inside the complex) 6.Meeting a monk 7.Seeing the Bayon at Angkor Thom

Petra , The Rose City

Petra, one of the New 7 Wonders of the World is a representation of Jordan and therefore, Jordan’s most visited tourist attraction and it also comes under one of the most famous historical places in the world.
Also known as the Rose City due to the color of the stone and the Lost City as the site remained unknown to the Western world until 1812. UNESCO even described the historical and archaeological city as “one of the most precious cultural properties of man’s cultural heritage”.
The Mummy Returns, Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade are the most iconic movies that were filmed at Petra.
Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade created an awareness of Petra and therefore it increased tourism of the destination.

Best time to visit:

Spring months of March-May and autumn months of September-November

Best things to do :

1.Riding a camel is a must 2.Hike through the Petra trails 3.Chill at the world’s first bar 4.Witness a magical Sunset

The Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

When we talk about historical places in the world, we really cannot forget “The Stonehenge”,
located in Wiltshire in England for it is one of the remarkable examples of prehistoric architecture. The structure is older than all the pyramids of Egypt and holds great religious significance for Neo-Druidry(a religion).
Stonehenge represents the spiritual connection of man and the elements which show the beliefs of the Druid community. The monument is made of two major types of stone: sarsens and bluestones. Sarsens are the larger ones with over 20 tons of weight. It was built with a sophisticated understanding of mathematics and geometry, as aligned with the rising and setting of the Sun, which is truly astounding.

Best time to visit :

If you really want to avoid busy days and big crowds, the best time of year to visit Stonehenge is Spring or Autumn. You can pick between April, May, or October. The weather is not as cold and rainy as during winter.

Best things to do :

1.Experience Neolithic houses 2.Enter a special historic exhibition between 9:30 – 17:00 hrs 3.Stonehenge Visitors Center: A Virtual Experience
